Selected Projects

Tactile Data in Imitation Learning Nov 2025 – Present
  • Research aiming to incorporate tactile data from human demonstrations to improve the retargeting process for robotic grippers in imitation learning.
  • Designed a tactile sensing glove to capture contact data in human manipulation demonstrations.
Formalizing Physics Proofs through Lean Nov 2025 – Present
  • Formulating Euclidean QFT axioms in Lean (Theorem Proving Language) with the assistance of LLMs.
  • Constructing proofs of a generalized free field satisfying the O-S Axioms.
  • Identified a major inconsistency in axiom definitions and reconstructed the proof after correction.
FM Radio Emitter & Receiver Oct 2025
  • Built an analog FM transmitter and receiver as a class project.
  • Tuned oscillators, filters, and amplifiers to achieve clean audio transmission.
3D Ising Model Bootstrap Dec 2023
  • Applied semidefinite programming (SDPB) to the conformal bootstrap to explore the allowed "island" region of the 3D Ising model.
  • Implemented robust numerical optimization pipelines to study bounds on conformal data.
